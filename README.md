## 💡 COMMON QUESTIONS

### "Do I have to fill out the entire daily log every day?"
**Short answer:** No.
**Long answer:** Focus on the metrics (focus/sleep/energy/mood) and routine checkboxes. The rest is optional. Fill in more detail when something noteworthy happens.

### "What if I miss a day?"
**Answer:** It's fine. Just fill out the next day's log with a note: "Missed yesterday." No need to backfill.

### "How do I know if a supplement is working?"
**Answer:** Compare your metrics from before you started (baseline) to now. Look for patterns over 2-4 weeks, not daily fluctuations.

### "Should I show this to my doctor?"
**Answer:** Yes! Bring `supplements-tracker.md` and your weekly summaries. Your doctor will love the data.

### "Can I customize the routine?"
**Answer:** Absolutely. This is YOUR routine. Adjust times, blocks, and supplements based on what works for your life.

## 🔧 CUSTOMIZATION

### Adjusting Wake Time
If 6:30 AM doesn't work for you:
1. Pick a time that does (7:00 AM, 7:30 AM, etc.)
2. Keep it consistent (±30 min max)
3. All other time blocks shift accordingly

### Adjusting Focus Blocks
If 90 minutes is too long:
1. Start with 25 minutes (Pomodoro)
2. Build up to 45, then 60, then 90
3. Quality beats quantity

### Adjusting Supplements
If you can't afford the full stack:
1. Start with foundation only (Omega-3 + protein)
2. Add based on bloodwork (Vit D3, etc.)
3. Skip the nice-to-haves (Rhodiola, L-Theanine)
